What we have to you?

Vanitas

The charming and elegant interiors of the signature Italian restaurant at Palazzo Versace Dubai, set against the Dubai Creek, acts as the perfect backdrop for an intimate and cosy experience or a romantic dinner. With an emphasis on classic Italian food, Vanitas is a restaurant with finesse, offering a range of expertly curated dishes.

Dining
